What is OPSEC?

Operations security (OPSEC) is a multidisciplinary approach for protecting information. Though the term has a wider general meaning, this guide discusses OPSEC in the context of Internet services: how to secure data against industrial espionage, blackhat hackers, law enforcement, social engineering and mass surveillance.

What is this guide?

OPSEC - Operations security is a guide and assessment tool for Internet service development. Developers, system administrators and decision-makers can learn to guarantee the safety of their projects against external and internal threats. The practical guide applies to one-man teams as well as large organizations building websites. A special emphasis is put on high-value targets like cryptocurrency services.
